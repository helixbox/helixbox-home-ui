002 // AI-Driven Intelligence Platform

Pubfi

PubFi is an AI-driven intelligence platform that restructures fragmented crypto information into clear, actionable insights. Powered by an end-to-end “intelligence supply chain,” PubFi ingests news, on-chain data, community signals, and prediction-market activity, then transforms them into precise, topic-based intelligence streams.

Its intelligence-mining engine uses ontology-inspired structuring to understand entities, events, and their relationships across the crypto landscape. By mapping signals to well-defined concepts and topics, PubFi filters noise, preserves context, and enables both AI and human editors to refine insights with higher accuracy. The result is a fast, reliable intelligence feed that helps users track narratives, interpret market movements, and make sharper decisions.
